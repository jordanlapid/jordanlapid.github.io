Street Fighter RPG
A Game in jQuery
Description
A “Street Fighter” themed RPG browser game. The player selects a character, selects an opponent, then clicks the fight button. Life totals are decremented randomly. If the player defeats the opponent, the player selects a new opponent. Defeat all other opponents to win!
Details
  • TechnologiesHTML, CSS, Javascript, jQuery
